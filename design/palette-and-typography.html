Typography — Selected
Heading: Source Serif 4 — weight 600 / weight 300 contrast, no italic.  Body: Nunito (warm, rounded, approachable). Body colour to be refined during implementation — may use a lighter shade than pure near-black.
Heading Source Serif 4 / Body DM Sans

1 — DM Sans body

The baseline. Clean, neutral, very legible. Slightly cool — the most common pairing for this heading style.

3 — Nunito body
