Typography Pairings — Revised
Previous round feedback: Playfair Display feels too "magazine grabby" — wants humble confidence, not showing off. Cormorant Garamond is alright but too skimpy. Fraunces not refined enough. Direction: refined, scholarly, warm — text serifs used at display sizes, not theatrical display faces.
